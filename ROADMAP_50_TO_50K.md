# ROADMAP_50_TO_50K

This is the Barbell Strategy Roadmap to go from $50 to $50,000.

## Core Principles
1. **Barbell Allocation**: 90% of portfolio in safe assets (e.g., stablecoin yields), 10% in high-risk high-reward plays (e.g., altcoin HFT based on funding rates).
2. **Funding Rate Arbitrage**: Exploit large positive or negative funding rates across exchanges.
3. **Strict Drawdown Limits**: Maximum 5% total drawdown allowed per day. Max exposure at any given point is dynamically capped.

## Phases
- **Phase 1: $50 -> $500**: Focus on minimizing fees and catching extreme funding rate anomalies on low-cap coins.
- **Phase 2: $500 -> $5,000**: Introduce cross-exchange basis trading.
- **Phase 3: $5,000 -> $50,000**: Scale up trade sizes, implement strict risk management via `RiskEngine`, and optimize latency.
